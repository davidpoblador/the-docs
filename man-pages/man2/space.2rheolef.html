DESCRIPTION

The space class contains some numbering for unknowns and blocked degrees of freedoms related to a given mesh and polynomial approximation.

SYNOPSIS

    space Q (omega, "P1");
    space V (omega, "P2", "vector");
    space T (omega, "P1d", "tensor");

PRODUCT

    space X = T*V*Q;
    space Q2 = pow(Q,2);

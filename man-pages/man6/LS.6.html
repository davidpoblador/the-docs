SYNOPSIS

LS [ -alFe ]

DESCRIPTION

LS Displays animations aimed to correct users who accidentally enter LS instead of ls.

OPTIONS

-a

An accident seems to happen. You'll feel pity for people who cry for help.

-l

shows little one.

-F

It flies.

-e

Allow interrupt by Ctrl+C.

RELATED TO LS…

sl(6),ls(1)

BUGS

It rarely shows contents of current directory.

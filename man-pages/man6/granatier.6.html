DESCRIPTION

Granatier is a clone of the classic Bomberman(TM) game, inspired by the work of the Clanbomber clone.

The object of the game is to run through an arena, using bombs to clear out blocks and eliminate your opponents. Several bonuses and handicaps are hidden underneath the blocks – these can either help or hinder your progress.

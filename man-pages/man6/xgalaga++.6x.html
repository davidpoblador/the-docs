SYNOPSIS

xgalaga++

DESCRIPTION

XGalaga++ is a classic vertical scrolling shoot'em up: fire at aliens, collect bonuses and survive as long as you can.

KEYBOARD COMMANDS

1,2,3,4,5

Resize the window to 640x480, 800x600, 1024x768, 1280x1024, or 1600x1200.

h

Toggle high scores and menu.

p

Pause.

q

End game, or quit XGalaga++ (from menu).

s

Start game

space

Fire.

left/rightarrow

Move vessel left/right.

up/downarrow

Scroll high scores up/down.

-/+

Decrease/increase details (use a bit less CPU and memory).

ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES

XGAL_PSEUDO - Player pseudonym, used for high scores.

lcd-flash (8)

Momentarily inverts the lcd to indicate an error

SYNOPSIS

lcd-flash [-s]

DESCRIPTION

Momentarily inverts the LCD display to indicate than an input error has occured.

--s

Indicates the flash should be performed silently. Don't print anything to STDOUT unless absolutely needed.

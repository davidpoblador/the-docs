SYNOPSIS

pytone [options]

DESCRIPTION

PyTone is a music jukebox written in Python, that helps you to play and organize your audio (ogg, mp3) files. It supports several external players (mpg321, mpg123, and xmms) as well as one internal player based on libmad and libvorbis, respectively.

OPTIONS

-h, --help shows this help

-c, --config <filename>

read config from <filename>

-d, --debug <filename>

enable debugging output (into <filename>)

-r, --rebuild

rebuild all databases

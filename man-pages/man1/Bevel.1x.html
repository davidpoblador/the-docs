CONFIGURATION OPTIONS

Bottom

Draw extra line at the bottom of the widget.

Extra

Adds extra thickness to 3D Bevel appearance.

Left

Draw extra line on the Left of the widget.

NoInline

Do not display the frame inside the widget - if used without any other option it will have the same effect as none.

NoOutline

Do not display the frame around the widget - if used without any other option it will have the same effect as none.

None

Do not draw anything around the widget.

Right

Draw extra line on the right of the widget.

Top

Draw extra line on top of the widget.

By default all bevels are on and default to Extra. Bevels are a simple way to produce a 3d effect for buttons and widgets in general. Defending on the options discribed below, this effect can be configured to your liking. Please note that multiple choices are possible, just separate the option with a comma.

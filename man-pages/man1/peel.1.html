SYNOPSIS

peel [-q] [-c] < TRACKS

DESCRIPTION

peel rips and encodes the tracks specified on standard input, using cdparanoia and (by default) oggenc.

OPTIONS

-q, --quiet

Do not play songs as they are ripped.

-c, --continue

Instead of invoking cdparanoia, read from WAV files in the current directory named as with cdparanoia -B.

EXAMPLES

mbget | vipe | peel

cdparanoia -B; mbget | peel -c

FILES

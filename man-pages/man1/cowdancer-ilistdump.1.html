SYNOPSIS

cowdancer-ilistdump ilist-path

DESCRIPTION

cowdancer-ilistdump Dumps the contents of .ilist file used in cowdancer.

EXAMPLES

cowdancer-ilistdump .ilist

dumps the content of .ilist file. Output is in the form of 'device number' 'inode number' for one file per line.

Also gives header information to stderr.

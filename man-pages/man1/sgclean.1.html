SYNOPSIS

sgclean configfile

DESCRIPTION

sgclean removes redundant entries in domain files and urls files.

sgclean always need explicit the `configfile' i.e. `/etc/squidguard/squidGuard.conf'.

Although sgclean makes a backup of the old files, it's always a good idea to make your own backup before running the program.

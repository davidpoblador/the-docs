DESCRIPTION

Nama is an application for multitrack recording, non-destructive editing, mixing and mastering using the Ecasound audio engine developed by Kai Vehmanen.

Features include tracks, buses, effects, presets, sends, inserts, marks and regions. Nama runs under \s-1JACK\s0 and \s-1ALSA\s0 audio frameworks, automatically detects \s-1LADSPA\s0 plugins, and supports Ladish Level 1 session handling.

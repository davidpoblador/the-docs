PURPOSE

To merge a range of cells in a spreadsheet

NODE NAME

\s-1MERGE_RANGE\s0

INHERITANCE

\s-1ELEMENT\s0

EFFECTS

This will merge a range of cells.

DEPENDENCIES

None

USAGE

  <cell ref="foo"/>
  <cell ref="foo"/>
  <cell ref="foo"/>
  <merge_range ref="foo">Text to insert into merged range</merge_range>

Or a cross rows:

<row> <cell ref="foo"/> <cell ref="foo"/> <cell ref="foo"/> </row> <row> <cell ref="foo"/> <cell ref="foo"/> <cell ref="foo"/> <format> <merge_range ref="foo">Text to insert into merged range</merge_range> </format> </row>
